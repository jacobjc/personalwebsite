concerning imagery

Imagery to me is the opportunity to share my perspective with the world. I do the usual engagement with imagery, social media, phone photos, etc, but I also have specialities that other may not. Specifically, I engage in a lot of cinematography and film work, and this is where I excel. I founded a production studio back at home (Kittery Point, Maine) and work on it with a few of my colleagues. I rely on imagery because it literally is part of my job and career. I want to continue to professionally create imagery in the future.
If I had to be in a gallery to show off my work, I would like it to be alongside other cinema creators. I few I personally like is Corridor Digital, Freddie Wong because of their online presence, but there are many other creators out there who do remarkable work and that I aspire to be like.
Most associate imagery with images / pictures, which isn’t wrong, but movies and animations are also a form of imagery, and the ones that I most associate myself with. Videos are still images, just usually twenty four of them a second. Technically, that might mean that videos have more imagery than a single image. I can confidently say the film defines me as a person, I think about it all the time, I love creating with it, and most of all I enjoy sharing my perspectives on life with other people. Allowing someone to see through someone else’s shoes, and to realize a world outside of their own, is in my opinion the best part of film. There are many aspects of film and I enjoy doing all of them, but for this class I can focus more in on the cinematography aspects because, well, this is an Image class.
