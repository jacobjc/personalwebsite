Final Project

“Not Here"

Artist Statement My Photos

Artist Statement

These images showcase a surrealistic view on internal conflict and external frustration. At the time of shooting, subjects Mikayleigh and Mark were amidst final exams for their Junior year of college, as well as a global pandemic. They were both asked to select six places where they would rather be. Inspired by the previous projects, Documentary, Portrait, and Man Ray, this project utilizes old and new artistic methods to express emotion. These consist of AI (edge-detection), Chroma Keying (green screens), and classic Black and White photography. Subjects deliberately have heavy contrast, whereas backgrounds have lighter contrast. This represents the separation between daydreams and our harsh reality.

My Photos

All photos were shot with a Sony a6500 mirrorless camera, and edited in Photoshop 2020. The files here are formatted for the web, you can access the originals on Google Drive.
