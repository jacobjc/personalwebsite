Design Challenge 4

What a Pretty Mess

Ideation Final Compositions

Ideation

I had to choose three of the seven deadly sins to focus on.

Firstly, what is this project?
The goal of DC4 was to create three composite images of the most compelling "sins" or "virtues" or "millennial cultural oddity" as I saw them. I chose to follow the original deadly sins, with my own interpretation of course.

Below, I have all seven deadly sins mapped out, which helped me choose which ones I wanted to focus on.

Final Compositions

Scroll down to see my final images and descriptions.

When I was creating all of these compositions, I needed to create a uniform look. To do this, I duplicated the layout of the comps, and methods of overlaying. Each has two bottom images representing me, overlaid with graphics. There also is a center image of myself, masked out and stroked with a pattern. Behind the mask, there is some duplicated items floating around, and behind that is the sin written out.

What separates these comps, is just the highlight coloring, and the images used. Put together, they all make up a uniform set.

The first sin I chose was Pride. I picked this one because I thought it went well with myself, as I take a lot of pride in the things I do. From my mindmap, I took the words happiness, awards, and fitness, and used them to craft my composite. For my bottom images, I chose a picture of me flexing overlaid with weights, and a picture of me at an awards show overlaid with a trophy. For my center image, I masked a portrait of me looking happy with myself. In the background, I have happy/cool face emojis, representing pride.

The second sin I chose was Greed. I picked this one because I think everyone's a little selfish from time to time, especially with valuables. From my mindmap, I took the words valuables, wealth, and selfish, and used them to craft my composite. For my bottom images, I chose a picture of my computer parts which are very expensive, overlaid with money, and a picture of my holding a $40k camera overlaid with a successive graph. For my center image, I masked a portrait of me looking happy with my success. I'd dare say I look a bit smug too. In the background, I have money falling from the sky, representing greed..

The final sin I chose was Wrath. I picked this one because I think wrath it a very interesting concept, and I thought it would be cool to try and represent. From my mindmap, I took the words anger, rage, pain, and violence, and used them to craft my composite. For my bottom images, I chose a picture of me running and screaming overlayed with a text swear censor (@#*%!!), and a picture of when I got my shoulder scar (sorry for the graphicness) overlaid with a gun and knife. For my center image, I masked a portrait of me looking, well, evil. In the background, I have flames and sparks flying around, representing wrath.
