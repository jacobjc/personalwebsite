About

We had to create a video, as a group, based off another piece of film.

Firstly, what is this project?
The goal of DC5 was to create a ~6 minute video which relies on both original and appropriated (downloaded) footage. Also, each group member had to have their own cut, that was ~5 minutes long.

About our video(s)
In order to challenge our shooting and editing abilities and exercise our creative thinking as storytellers, we decided to make our own episode in the style of Black Mirror. Based loosely off of the episode Hang the DJ, our video uses original scenes as well as some appropriated music and scenes.

In our version of the story, we follow three characters as they repeatedly relive a normal day on campus. Viewers are left to wonder, what is the mysterious technology that is causing their day to repeat endlessly?

We don't want to spoil anything, so a more descriptive analysis is written below the final video.

Final Cut:

About Expiration Date   (Spoilers!)
We all love Black Mirror, and we wanted to create something that lived up to its name.

Firstly, we had to include Anyone Who Knows What Love Is (Will Understand), a popular callback song use in many episodes. For appropriated content, we weren't sure what to use, so we relied on music. The Hang the DJ soundtrack was used the most, but we also included Another One Bites the Dust and There She Goes (credits) because Black Mirror commonly uses upbeat pop songs over ominous stories.

If you can follow the plot, which might be tricky, the three of us in the video are trying to submit this assignment. Super meta. Problem is, we keep dying and can't reach the point where we upload it, until the end of the video. Finally, it is revealed that it was all a simulation, and in fact Chegg was testing to see how likely we would get the project done in time, the day before it was due.

I hope you enjoyed, I had a lot of fun creating Expiration Date. My individual cut is below, it's more rough, but feel free to check it out if you're interested.
