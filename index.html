About:

Jacob Christiansen is an award-winning filmmaker from Kittery Point, Maine. He found initial success with his student films “Vacationland" and “The Way Life Should Be".  In 2018, he co-founded Seapoint Studios with the goal of bringing more indie film to New England.  Jacob went on to produce and edit the award-winning short films, “The Squirrels in the Attic" , “In the Company of Crows" & “Penny".

In 2021, he graduated with honors from the University of Colorado Boulder with a Bachelor's Degree. Studying in both Technical Arts and Computer Science programs, he explored how integral tech, video, and the arts can be.

Shortly after leaving school, Jacob moved to Denver and began working full-time at Guerilla Capturing. He worked with their team to produce & edit high-budget videos for industry-leading corporations, such as Amazon, Microsoft, Twitter, and Lincoln. He was promoted to Creative Director and continues working there today.
